What is a Secret Deal?

There will be many instances when a logged in user might visit your hotel page and not do a booking.

To get this user to book your hotel, you need to send some attractive deals, and that can be done by creating a Secret Deal.

This deal will not be visible on the hotel page but will be sent only to the user through his email.

Note: Secret Deals shall be applied on top of existing offers. Eg: Your sell price is Rs.1000/- and there is a regular discount offer of 10%. If you provide a secret deal of 10%, it would be applied on top of Rs.900/-, and the final sell price would be Rs.810/- (0.9x900).
