Overview:

Casa Baga, Goa, features front desk, room service, Wi-Fi, parking. The cozy in-house restaurant and bar is a perfect place to pamper the taste buds with a wide range of delectable dishes and drinks. The rooms are equipped with air-conditioner, television, intercom. They are spacious and decorated in warm neutrals and textures. They have attached bathrooms fitted with necessary toiletries. The proximity of hotel to numerous tourist attractions makes it one of the most sought after accommodation options in Goa. Guests can explore places like St Alex Church(1 km), Tibetan Market(1 km), Calangute Beach(2 km), Baga Beach(2 km), Holiday Street(2 km) and other places. Shoppers can have fun-filled shopping experience at nearby mall. The hotel is located at a distance of 18 km from Thivim Railway Station and 38 km from Goa International Airport. Relax and have a lovely stay at Casa Baga Resort!
